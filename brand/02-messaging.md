## Value Proposition (Three-part structure)

### The Problem
Commercial property owners overpay on insurance and energy, let rents drift below market, and miss income streams sitting on their own rooftops — not because they're bad investors, but because no single tool shows them the full picture. Their advisors are paid on transactions, not on outcomes. The software they use reports problems. Nobody fixes them.

### The Solution
Arca connects every data point across your portfolio — costs, rents, compliance, market rates, acquisitions pipeline — and works backwards from what each asset should be earning. When it finds a gap, it closes it: places insurance directly with carriers, switches energy suppliers, flags rent reviews before they're missed, identifies 5G masts and EV charging income your assets are already sitting on. Every action is one click. Every outcome is tracked.

### The Model
We take a percentage of what we save or generate. No subscription. No setup fee. No invoice until you're ahead. That's not a pricing decision — it's a conviction that the product works.

## Tone of Voice

### Principles

**Direct.** Never say "we recommend you consider." Say "Arca switches this."

**Specific.** Numbers over adjectives. "£18,000 saving" not "significant savings."

**Active.** The subject is always Arca or the asset, never "it was found that..."

**Confident without arrogance.** We know this works. We don't need to oversell it.

**Peer-level.** We're talking to experienced investors who know their numbers. Don't explain what a cap rate is. Don't add unnecessary caveats.

### Voice in practice

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| "Our AI-powered platform leverages machine learning to..." | "Arca finds the gap. Then closes it." |
| "You may be able to save on insurance costs." | "You're overpaying £14,200 on insurance. Arca places it direct." |
| "We offer a commission-based model for our clients." | "No fee until you're ahead." |
| "Our comprehensive dashboard provides insights into..." | "Every asset. Every cost. One screen." |
| "Would you like to learn more?" | "See your portfolio." |

## Market-Specific Messaging

### Florida (FL) — Owner-Operators
- Lead with numbers: G2N benchmarks, dollar savings, IRR
- Emphasise speed: "Live in 48 hours, first saving within 30 days"
- Stress independence: "No advisors. No middlemen. Your asset, your call."
- Hurricane/FEMA risk: surface compliance and insurance placement as critical

### South-East UK — Owner-Operators
- Lead with rent: ERV drift is the most painful, most familiar problem
- Emphasise the institutional comparison: "The intelligence of a £500M fund, for your 8-asset portfolio"
- Planning Intelligence is highly differentiated in SE UK — mention it
- Tone slightly more understated than FL; let the numbers speak
