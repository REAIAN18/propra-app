3 Energy Intelligence — Full Specification

Beyond tariff switching: consumption analysis, anomaly detection, waste identification, upgrade recommendations

What this is

RealHQ does not just switch energy suppliers. It analyses how each building uses energy, identifies where it is being wasted or misused, calculates the saving from fixing each issue, and either executes the fix automatically (tariff switch, scheduling change) or commissions the physical work (lighting upgrade, HVAC replacement) through the procurement workflow. This is energy management software, not an energy broker.

4 Insurance — Premium Reduction Strategies

Not just quoting — how to reduce the premium itself before and after placement

What this adds

The previous spec covered getting live quotes from carriers. This section adds what comes before the quote: how RealHQ analyses each property and portfolio to identify actions that reduce the premium the carriers will charge — and shows the owner exactly what each action costs, how much it reduces the premium, and how to get it done.



4.1 How RealHQ assesses current insurance position

  • On insurance schedule upload: AI extracts policy type, insured values, excess levels, claims history, property construction, age, EPC, flood zone, security specification

  • Each factor is scored against carrier risk appetite — RealHQ knows what each carrier prices each factor at

  • Output: risk factor scorecard showing which elements are driving premium up and by how much

  • Benchmark: premium compared to market rate for same risk profile from carrier API data

4.3 How RealHQ gets to the premium figure

  • Step 1: Schedule upload → AWS Textract extracts current premium, insured value, property details, excess, endorsements

  • Step 2: Risk scoring → AI maps each property characteristic to carrier risk factors (FEMA zone, construction type, EPC, security, claims history, age, use class)

  • Step 3: Market benchmark → carrier APIs return live quotes for current risk profile. Gap = current premium minus cheapest live quote.

  • Step 4: Improvement modelling → for each reduction action above, RealHQ recalculates the premium the carrier would charge AFTER the improvement. Shows: action cost, new quote, annual saving, payback.

  • Step 5: Ranked actions → presented as "Do these three things in this order to reduce your annual premium from £142,000 to £89,000 over 18 months"

  • Step 6: Execution → each action links to its execution workflow (procurement for physical works, policy restructuring for consolidation, surveyor for reinstatement)

  • Step 7: Placement → when owner is ready, RealHQ places with the carrier returning the best quote for the improved risk profile. Commission: 15% of year-1 saving.

8 Acceptance Criteria — Energy & Insurance

Energy Intelligence — done when:

  • Connect a smart meter for a test property — half-hourly reads appear on the consumption heatmap within 5 minutes

  • Upload a utility bill — tariff is extracted, supplier API is called, live comparison appears within 30 seconds. No illustrative data.

  • Overnight HVAC waste is detected automatically from smart meter data and appears as an anomaly card with correct saving calculation

  • LED upgrade recommendation shows: install cost from contractor panel, annual saving from current kWh + rate data, payback period, EPC improvement

  • "Switch" on the tariff comparison executes the contract switch via supplier API. Owner receives confirmation. No phone call happens.

  • Carbon reporting panel shows kgCO2e/sqft for each property with CRREM pathway comparison



Insurance Intelligence — done when:

  • Upload an insurance schedule — all policy details extracted correctly (premium, insured value, excess, renewal date, carrier, exclusions)

  • Risk scorecard shows which factors are driving the premium with quantified impact

  • Live quotes from at least 3 carriers appear within 60 seconds — clearly marked as binding or indicative, with timestamp

  • Premium reduction roadmap shows at least 3 actions with cost, saving, payback for a typical commercial property

  • "Get quotes" on LED or alarm upgrade links to the contractor procurement workflow with RealHQ sourcing installers

  • Reinstatement value survey can be ordered from RICS panel API within the Insurance screen

  • Placing a policy generates a confirmation, document in vault, renewal reminder set, commission recorded
