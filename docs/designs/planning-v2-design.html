1. Planning Policy Analysis

Zoning & Density
The site is zoned C-MX2 (Mixed Use Corridor) under the Coral Gables Zoning Code. This permits office, retail, and residential uses up to 5 storeys / 75 ft. Current built FAR is 2.6; maximum permitted FAR is 3.0, leaving 0.4 FAR (~4,200 sq ft) of additional development capacity without requiring a variance.
Historic District Overlay
The property is outside the Coral Gables Historic District boundary but within the Coral Gables Design Review zone. Any external alterations require Board of Architects approval. This adds 4–8 weeks to the planning timeline and requires design sympathetic to the Mediterranean Revival character of the area.
Parking & Access
Current parking provision: 32 spaces (1 per 330 sq ft). Code requires 1 per 400 sq ft for office, 1 per 300 sq ft for residential. Adding 8,400 sq ft office requires 21 additional spaces. Options: structured parking, valet service, or in-lieu fee ($12,000/space).
