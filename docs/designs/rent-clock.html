Key design principles:
• Large countdown number — days not dates. "90 days" is visceral, "Sep 2026" is abstract.
• Cost of inaction number is mandatory — not just a deadline, a money figure.
• Review type badge on every card: Open market / Fixed increase / CPI / Index-linked
• Fixed increase: show automatic uplift AND flag that it prevents capturing full market uplift
• Backdating clause: flag if present — act before the review date
• Urgency tiers: red <90d · amber 90–180d · green 180d+
• After approval: track response, nudge if no reply in 5 working days
