## Most property software tells you what's wrong. Arca fixes it.

---

### The problem

Owner-operators with 3–30 commercial assets are running blind.

- **25–35% insurance overpay** — renewal-rolled policies, no retender, carriers reward inertia
- **18% energy overpay** — legacy commercial tariffs vs what's available in market today
- **10–20% rent below ERV** — leases roll without review prep; income drifts quietly
- **Missed income** — 5G masts, EV charging, solar, subletting. The contracts exist. Most owners have never been asked.
- **No real G2N view** — you know your rent roll. You don't know your net yield vs comparable portfolios.

The result: a 10-asset portfolio that should net $280k/yr is netting $240k. The gap is invisible until someone maps it.

---

### What Arca does

Arca connects your portfolio data to live market benchmarks, finds the gaps, and goes and fixes them.

**Insurance** — We retender your commercial policies through our carrier panel (Lloyd's, AIG, Chubb, Travelers, Aviva). We place the saving.

**Energy** — We benchmark your contracts against live commercial tariffs and execute the switch. You approve, we handle it.

**Rent roll** — We map every lease against current ERV, flag the gaps, and prepare the review case before the deadline.

**Additional income** — We identify 5G, EV, solar, and subletting opportunities on your specific assets and manage the operator relationship to signature.

**Acquisitions** — AI-powered deal scoring against your acquisition criteria. We flag the opportunities; you decide.
