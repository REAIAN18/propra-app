## Outreach Link Personalization

Replace the `/book` link in Touch 3 with a personalized URL for each prospect.

**Format:** `https://arcahq.ai/book?name=[FIRST+LAST]&company=[COMPANY+NAME]&assets=[N]`

**Example:** `https://arcahq.ai/book?name=John+Smith&company=Smith+Industrial+LLC&assets=8`

- Shows the prospect a personalized booking page (their name + company pre-filled)
- Pre-fills the cal.com embed with their name
- Fires a lead capture event to Arca — **Ian gets an email notification when the prospect clicks the link**
- Tracks which prospects engage with the outreach

## Targeting Notes

**Where to find them:**
- CoStar/LoopNet ownership records for FL industrial and mixed-use
- LinkedIn: search "commercial property" + "Florida" + "owner" / "portfolio" / "investments"
- County property appraiser records (public, searchable by owner for FL)
- Local BOMA Florida chapter membership lists
- NAIOP Florida chapter (members tend to be active investors, not just brokers)

**Prioritise:**
- Owners with 5–15 assets (sweet spot for our commission math)
- Assets held 3+ years (more likely on legacy insurance/energy contracts)
- Mixed-use and industrial (higher energy intensity = bigger energy savings opportunity)
- No institutional management company listed (means they're doing it themselves = our target)
