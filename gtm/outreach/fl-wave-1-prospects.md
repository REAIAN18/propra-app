## FL Market Context (for calls)

- **Insurance:** FL commercial premiums up 40–60% since 2020 (FAIA data). Hurricane Ian (2022), Helene and Milton (2024) have driven further carrier exits. Most owner-operators are on individual per-asset placements — consolidating 5+ assets onto one schedule saves 15–25%.
- **Energy:** FPL (SE FL), Duke Energy FL (Tampa/Central FL), TECO (Hillsborough). Legacy GS tariff contracts from 2021–2022 are now 15–20% above current commercial market. EIA FL commercial: ~10.8–11.4c/kWh Q4 2024. Contracts locked at 12–14c/kWh in 2022 are most overpaying.
- **Income:** Solar — FL sun yield 1,400–1,600 kWh/kWp/yr (NREL data); PPA income $80–120k/yr for 500kWp rooftop. EV charging — ChargePoint, Blink, Tesla Destination: $3,200–$4,800/bay/yr for owner. 5G masts — AT&T, T-Mobile, Crown Castle: $18–28k/yr for urban/suburban FL industrial rooftop.
