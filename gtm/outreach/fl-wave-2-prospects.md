## FL Sub-Market Context — Wave 2 Focus Areas

- **Palm Beach County:** Palm Beach County commercial insurance up 30–45% since 2022. FPL legacy GS-2 commercial tariffs locked 2021–2022 typically 10–15% above current market. North Palm Beach industrial (Jupiter/Palm Beach Gardens) is still growing with significant spec development — new builds rarely have optimised energy procurement.
- **Broward fringe (Davie, Weston, SW Ranches):** Western Broward industrial saw less hurricane direct damage than coastal assets but still faced broker-driven premium increases. Multi-unit flex parks in Davie/Weston corridor hold highest concentration of legacy per-unit contracts.
- **Miami-Dade north (Hialeah, Medley, Opa-locka, Miami Gardens):** Densest private industrial ownership in FL. Highest energy intensity sub-market (manufacturing, cold chain). Most legacy insurance placements — multi-generational ownership with same regional brokers. Insurance repricing 40–60% since 2020. Biggest opportunity per asset.
