# Prospecting Playbook — Florida Owner-Operators
**Goal:** 50 qualified names in the FL pipeline within 5 working days
**Target:** Owner-operators, 3–30 commercial assets, industrial / mixed-use / logistics, FL
**Output:** Fill `sales-materials/gtm/prospects-fl.csv`

---

## Source 1 — Florida County Property Appraiser Records (Free, Public, Fast)
*Best source for actual ownership data. Bypasses brokers entirely.*

**Priority counties (industrial/mixed-use concentration):**
1. **Miami-Dade** — miamidade.gov/pa (search by owner, filter commercial)
2. **Broward** — bcpa.net (owner search, non-homesteaded commercial)
3. **Palm Beach** — pbcgov.com/papa
4. **Orange** (Orlando) — ocpafl.org
5. **Hillsborough** (Tampa) — hcpafl.org
6. **Pinellas** (St. Pete) — pcpao.gov
7. **Duval** (Jacksonville) — coj.net/departments/property-appraiser

**How to search:**
1. Go to county PA website → "Search by owner name"
2. Filter to: property type = commercial / industrial / mixed-use
3. Look for owners with **3+ properties** under the same owner name or LLC
4. Note: many are held in LLCs — Google the LLC name + "Florida" to find the principal

**What to record:**
- Owner name (or LLC principal if you can find it)
- Number of assets in county
- Asset types + rough addresses
- Any contact info visible on corporate docs

**LLC lookback — Sunbiz.org:**
1. Take the LLC name from the PA record
2. Go to search.sunbiz.org → search the LLC name
3. Registered Agent or Officer list often has the owner's name and sometimes email
4. Cross-reference with LinkedIn to find the person

**Time per prospect:** ~8–12 min with practice
**Expected yield:** 15–20 qualified names per county searched

## Source 2 — LinkedIn Sales Navigator
*Best for finding decision-maker contact info and validating ownership*

**Search string 1 (FL industrial owners):**
```
Title: "owner" OR "director" OR "principal" OR "founder" OR "president"
Company keywords: "properties" OR "investments" OR "real estate" OR "industrial" OR "commercial"
Location: Florida
```

**Search string 2 (family office / portfolio angle):**
```
Title: "portfolio" OR "asset manager" OR "property manager" OR "managing director"
Keywords: "commercial real estate" OR "industrial" OR "mixed use"
Location: Florida
Seniority: Director, VP, CXO, Owner
```

**Filter refinements:**
- 2nd-degree connections only (warmer — mutual connection = credibility)
- Company headcount 1–20 (owner-managed, not institutional)
- Exclude: "CBRE", "JLL", "Cushman", "Colliers", "Newmark" (these are brokers not owners)

**How to qualify from LinkedIn:**
- Check their "About" section for asset count clues ("portfolio of X properties", "10+ years")
- Check company page for location listings
- Look at their activity — do they post about deals, renovations, tenant news?

**Connection note to use:**
> Hi [Name] — I help FL commercial property owners benchmark their insurance and energy costs vs market and find income they're not capturing. Happy to connect.

**Time per prospect:** ~5 min
**Expected yield:** 20–30 qualified connections in a focused 2hr session

## Source 3 — NAIOP Florida + BOMA Florida Member Directories
*Members are active, serious investors — self-selected qualification*

**NAIOP Florida chapters:**
- NAIOP South Florida (Miami/Broward/Palm Beach) — naiopsfl.org
- NAIOP Central Florida (Orlando/Tampa) — naiop.org/chapters

**Action:**
1. Attend a chapter event (or find the membership list via chapter website)
2. Most chapters publish sponsor/member lists publicly
3. Cross-reference member companies with county PA records to validate asset count

**BOMA Florida:**
- boma.org/find-a-local-association → Florida chapters
- Building Owners and Managers — directly our target profile
- Annual conference roster is often published

## Source 4 — LoopNet + CoStar (if you have access)
*Most accurate source for portfolio size — requires subscription*

**LoopNet (free tier):**
- Search "for sale" commercial properties → filter by owner type "private"
- Click seller profile → often shows other listings by same owner

**CoStar (paid):**
- Owner lookup: Property > Ownership > filter by state FL, property count 3+
- Export to CSV directly
- Segment by: asset type, last acquisition date (recent = active buyer = good prospect)

## Source 5 — Local CRE Press + Deal Announcements
*Identifies active owners who just bought/sold — high intent*

**Outlets to monitor:**
- South Florida Business Journal — bizjournals.com/southflorida
- Tampa Bay Business Journal — bizjournals.com/tampabay
- Orlando Business Journal — bizjournals.com/orlando
- GlobeSt Florida section — globest.com
- RE Business Online FL — rejournals.com

**What to look for:**
- "X acquires industrial portfolio in [city]"
- "[Owner] sells mixed-use property for $Xm"
- Anyone mentioned as buyer/seller of 2+ assets in 12 months

**Action:** Google "[city] commercial real estate acquisition 2024 2025" → read deal coverage → extract owner names

## Qualification Criteria (before adding to CSV)

**Green light (high priority):**
- 5–15 assets
- Industrial, logistics, or mixed-use
- Owner-managed (no major PM firm in the picture)
- Assets held 2+ years (likely on legacy insurance/energy)
- Based in FL or identifiable FL contact

**Yellow (lower priority but worth reaching):**
- 3–4 assets (smaller commission, but faster close and case study potential)
- Has a PM but owner is still engaged in finance/strategy decisions
- Out-of-state owner with FL assets (often less attentive to FL market rates)

**Red (skip):**
- Institutional ownership (REIT, fund, pension)
- Single asset only
- Pure residential (no commercial component)
- Developer with no standing portfolio
