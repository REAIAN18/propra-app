## Why referral partners

A warm intro from a trusted advisor converts at 3–5x the rate of a cold email. Accountants and surveyors already have the relationship; we give them a commission-free service to offer their clients. The pitch is not "sell Arca" — it's "here's something worth flagging to your commercial property clients."

## Notes on referral partner conversion

- **Expected conversion:** 1–2 of 10 will actively refer within 60 days. A single referral partner who introduces one commercial owner/quarter is worth 3–4 warm leads/year.
- **FL accountants** convert fastest — commercial property is a core client base and the referral fee is tangible. Lead with the numbers (insurance overpay, energy gap) rather than the programme mechanics.
- **SE UK surveyors** convert more slowly — they want to understand the service before referring clients. Send the one-pager and offer a 15-minute walkthrough. MEES 2027 is the fastest hook.
- **Don't over-explain.** The pitch is: "Your commercial property clients are probably overpaying. I find and fix it on a no-win, no-fee basis. You earn 2% of what we generate." That's it.
