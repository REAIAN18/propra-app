## SECTION 5 — UK-SPECIFIC HOOKS (use in personalisation and follow-up)

**Energy — post-Ofgem chaos:**
- Many SE businesses are on legacy fixed-term contracts that rolled over at peak 2022/23 rates
- Wholesale electricity down significantly since Q4 2023 — gap between legacy and new-deal rates is now large
- Hook: *"A lot of businesses auto-renewed at peak 2022/23 rates and haven't retendererd since. That's the overpay we keep finding."*

**MEES 2027 deadline:**
- From April 2027, commercial properties must be EPC B or above to be let (currently E)
- Owner-operators with D or E-rated units face either retrofit spend or void risk
- Arca can identify which units are at risk and model solar/efficiency works as income (FIT/smart export tariff)
- Hook: *"We flag MEES exposure as part of every audit — the ones that need work are often the same ones where we find the energy savings."*

**Insurance — Lloyd's and specialist market:**
- Commercial property insurance for logistics/industrial placed via Lloyd's specialist syndicates tends to beat standard broker markets by 15–25%
- Most owner-operators are with regional brokers on auto-renewal
- Hook: *"We go to the specialist Lloyd's market directly. Most regional brokers don't."*

**5G mast income:**
- MBNL, Cornerstone (Vodafone/O2 JV), and VMO2 actively seeking SE England logistics/industrial rooftop sites
- Typical: £8k–£15k/yr per site for rooftop access + £3k–£6k/yr for ground-level small cells
- Logistics parks on elevated positions or near motorway junctions are highest priority sites
