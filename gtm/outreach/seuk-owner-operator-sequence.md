## Targeting Notes

**Where to find them:**
- Land Registry ownership data for SE England commercial titles
- EGi / CoStar UK ownership and lease data
- LinkedIn: "commercial property" + "Surrey" / "Kent" / "Essex" / "Hertfordshire" + "owner" / "director" / "investor"
- RICS Matrics SE chapter for younger investors
- BPF (British Property Federation) member lists
- Local chambers of commerce (Kent Invicta, Surrey Chambers, etc.)

**Prioritise:**
- Logistics and industrial owners (high energy intensity, strong 5G/EV opportunity)
- Assets with leases 18+ months from expiry (lead time for rent review preparation)
- Owner-managed, no external property manager listed
- Family office structures or Ltd company names (not PLC or institutional)
