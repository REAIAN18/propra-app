# Arca Referral Programme
**For:** Surveyors, solicitors, accountants, mortgage brokers, financial advisers, property managers

## The Proposition

Your clients own commercial property. They're probably overpaying on insurance and energy, and they have income sitting on their assets they've never captured.

When you introduce them to Arca, we go and fix that — commission-only, no cost to them unless we deliver.

You earn 2% of everything we make from that client for the first 12 months. No cap. No minimum. No admin.

## How It Works

1. You introduce a commercial property owner to Arca (email, phone, or direct intro)
2. We run the portfolio health check and identify the opportunity
3. If they proceed, we agree terms and start executing
4. You receive 2% of all commissions generated from that client for 12 months

**Example:**
- Client: 8-asset Florida mixed-use portfolio
- Arca year-1 commissions: $8,400 (insurance + energy + rent review)
- Your referral fee: $168 for the year

On a better portfolio:
- Arca year-1 commissions: $22,000
- Your referral fee: $440

Fees are paid monthly, alongside our own commission settlements.
