## What Arca does — from engagement to invoice

### Week 1 — Data in, analysis built

You send us your portfolio data (asset list, insurance, energy, rent roll). We build your custom dashboard and run the full analysis across every asset.

What we're looking for:
- Insurance overpay vs direct market rates
- Energy spend vs current live tariffs
- Rent gaps — passing vs ERV, and every upcoming review window
- Additional income your assets are already positioned to earn (EV charging, 5G, rooftop solar)
- Compliance exposures — certificates, expiries, gaps

**You'll see all of it in one place, with a number next to each gap.**

---

### Weeks 2–4 — First actions

We start with whatever has the fastest payback. Usually insurance — retenders move in days, not months.

For each action:
1. Arca identifies the opportunity and the fix
2. You see it in your dashboard with the projected saving/income
3. You approve (or not — your call, always)
4. Arca executes: contacts carriers, suppliers, or operators on your behalf
5. You see the confirmed saving appear in your dashboard

Nothing is placed, switched, or signed without your explicit approval. We act in an advisory and coordination capacity. You retain full control.

---

### Weeks 4–12 — Ongoing optimisation

Once the quick wins are in, we move to the longer cycle items: rent reviews, energy contract timing, additional income negotiations, compliance gap closure.

Your dashboard updates as each action completes. The G2N (Gross-to-Net) tracker shows the cumulative gap between what your portfolio earns today and what it should earn — and how much of that gap we've closed.

## How Arca is paid

Commission only. We invoice when an action completes and a saving or income is confirmed.

| Action | Commission |
|---|---|
| Insurance saving | 15% of Year-1 annual saving |
| Energy saving | 10% of Year-1 annual saving |
| Rent uplift | 8% of Year-1 uplift above previous passing rent |
| New income streams | 10% of Year-1 income generated |
| Other cost reductions | 8% of Year-1 annualised saving |

**If nothing is saved or generated, nothing is invoiced.** No retainer, no setup fee, no minimum.
