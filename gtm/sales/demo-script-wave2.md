## PART 1 — Scout: Acquisitions Intelligence (LIVE NOW)

**Navigate to:** `/scout`

**Opening line:**

> "This is the part of the platform that watches the acquisition market for you — so you don't have to check LoopNet every day. It pulls live commercial deals, screens them against your criteria, and tells you which ones are worth a second look."

**[Screen: Scout grid view — deal cards with asset type, price, cap rate, days on market]**

> "These are live deals from LoopNet, updated hourly. You can see asking price, cap rate where disclosed, and days on market. The signal bar — 1 to 5 — tells you how much data we have on a deal: price, cap rate, size, broker, and recency. A 5-bar deal is well-documented; a 1-bar deal is thin."

**[Click on a deal to expand it]**

> "When you expand a deal you get the full picture — cap rate, square footage, broker, source. And these two buttons: Like it or Pass. If you like it, it drops into your acquisition pipeline. If you pass, it disappears."

**[Show reaction buttons]**

> "The platform is learning your criteria. The more you react, the better it gets at surfacing relevant deals."

### When underwriting goes live (Sprint 1 end):

> "And this is what's coming in a few weeks — [click Underwrite button if live]. The platform runs a full underwriting in under a second: NOI estimate, debt service coverage ratio at 65% LTV, 5-year IRR. If the numbers work, it generates a Letter of Intent draft you can download and send. Commission-only — we only earn when a deal closes."

---

**Scout qualification questions (use in conversation):**

> "Are you actively looking to acquire right now, or is this more opportunistic?"

*If actively looking:* → "What's your criteria — geography, asset type, size range? I can filter the feed to only surface those."

*If opportunistic:* → "This is perfect for you then. You don't have to do anything — if something that matches your portfolio crosses the feed, we surface it. No manual searching."

## PART 2 — Valuation Engine / AVM (COMING Sprint 2)

> "Coming soon — the platform will give you a live valuation for each asset in your portfolio, updated quarterly. Not a desktop appraisal, not a broker opinion — a data-driven model using comparable sales from the Land Registry [UK] or ATTOM Data [US], cross-checked against your current rent and the market cap rate."

**Why this matters:**

> "Most owners have a vague idea of what their assets are worth. We give them a number, a confidence interval, and — crucially — a gap between current value and ERV-adjusted value. That gap is the Hold vs Sell decision made visible."

---

## PART 3 — Hold vs Sell (COMING Sprint 3)

> "Every owner asks at some point: should I be selling this asset, or holding it? The platform models both scenarios — not a spreadsheet, a live model using your actual rent, your actual capex, the market exit yield for your asset class, and a 10-year DCF. It gives you: hold IRR, sell proceeds, NPV of holding vs selling today, and a recommendation with confidence score."

**The ask:**

> "If you have an asset where that question is live — you're not sure whether to refinance, sell, or keep — that's worth a 30-minute session with the model. We run it together, put the numbers on the table, and you decide."

## PART 4 — Tenant Intelligence Wave 2 (COMING Sprint 2)

> "The tenant intelligence already shows you health scores and expiry risk. What's coming next: the platform auto-generates a rent review letter when you hit the 12-month trigger window. You review it, it goes to the tenant, DocuSign captures the Heads of Terms. No surveyor, no negotiation service, no third party. You keep the uplift."

**Commission:** 8% of the achieved rent increase in year one.

> "On a £100k lease with a 10% uplift, our fee is £800. You pocket £9,200 increase for the year."

---

## PART 5 — Work Orders with Tender (COMING Sprint 2)

> "When you raise a maintenance job, the platform generates a scope of works using AI — line items, trade required, cost estimate. It then invites 3 contractors from the regional panel to tender. You pick the best quote, they start, and you track milestones. Payment on completion via GoCardless. We take 3% of the job cost — on a £20k refurb that's £600, and you've saved a week of calls."

---

## PART 6 — Planning Intelligence (COMING Sprint 3)

> "Every week, the platform checks the local planning portal for applications near your assets. If something comes in that affects value — a permitted development, a change of use, a major scheme — you get an alert. Classified automatically as positive, neutral, or negative. Most owners find out about planning impacts from their surveyor, months later. We tell you the week it files."

## Notes

- **Don't pre-announce features that aren't live.** Scout is live. Reference Wave 2 features as "coming in the next few weeks" not as present capabilities. If a prospect asks for a specific feature today, be honest about the timeline.
- **Scout is the strongest new hook for active acquirers.** LoopNet is a £2,000/yr subscription. We surface the same deals for free as part of the platform.
- **Rent review is the strongest hook for stable portfolios with upcoming lease events.** Ask "when does your next lease event hit?" — if it's within 18 months, that's the warm lead.
- **Hold vs Sell is the hook for owners considering an exit.** If they mention refinancing, selling, or that the portfolio is "getting to a point," that's the signal.
