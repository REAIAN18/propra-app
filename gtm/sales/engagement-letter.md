## What you are authorising

By signing this letter, you authorise Arca to act on your behalf to:

1. Contact your current insurance broker(s) and request a full market retender
2. Contact your current energy supplier(s) and request tariff comparisons
3. Approach third-party insurers, brokers, and energy suppliers for competitive quotes
4. Review your rent roll and prepare rent review analysis on nominated assets
5. Approach operators regarding ancillary income opportunities (EV charging, 5G, solar, subletting) on assets you identify

**You retain full approval rights on every action.** Nothing is placed, switched, or signed without your explicit instruction. Arca acts in an advisory and coordination capacity only.

---

## How Arca is paid

Arca earns commission only when a recommendation is implemented:

| Service | Commission | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Insurance saving | 15% | Year-1 annual premium saving |
| Energy saving | 10% | Year-1 annual contract saving |
| Rent uplift | 8% | Year-1 annual uplift above previous passing rent |
| New income streams | 10% | Year-1 income generated |
| Cost reduction (other) | 8% | Year-1 annualised saving |
| Acquisition advisory | 0.5–1% | Agreed per transaction |

**If no savings or income are realised, Arca earns nothing.** There is no retainer, setup fee, or minimum commitment.

Commission is invoiced on completion of each action (e.g. when new insurance policy is bound, when energy switch goes live). Payment terms: 30 days.

---

## Term and termination

This agreement covers a rolling 90-day period from the date of signing, renewable by mutual agreement.

Either party may terminate with 14 days' written notice. Any commissions earned on actions completed before termination remain payable.

---

## Confidentiality

Arca will treat all portfolio data, financial information, and contract terms shared by the client as strictly confidential. This information will not be shared with any third party except as required to obtain quotes or execute services (e.g., sharing insurance schedule with carriers for retender purposes).
