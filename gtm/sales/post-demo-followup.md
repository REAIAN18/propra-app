## Usage Notes

- **Personalise every number.** Do not send with placeholder text. Pull from the scan shown on the call.
- **One CTA only.** Either "send me the policy schedule" or "I'll follow up in [Month]." Not both.
- **No attachments** on the first follow-up — keep the reply barrier low.
- **Subject line** stays the same regardless of variant. Short, personal, no punctuation tricks.
- **If they mentioned a specific asset or situation on the call** — reference it in the email. Shows you were listening.
- **Send from** the same email address used in the outreach sequence so the thread is recognisable.
