Arca is a commercial real estate intelligence platform for private property owners with 5–30 assets across Florida and South-East UK.

Arca does both.

That's not a pricing gimmick. It's a structural commitment to your outcome. When we only get paid when the saving lands, we're incentivised to actually deliver it — not report it and move on.

Average uplift across the portfolios we've analysed: over £400,000 per year.

What a 5-asset Florida commercial portfolio typically misses.

We ran a gap analysis on a 5-asset retail and light industrial portfolio in the Tampa Bay area. Owner had held the assets for 6–9 years. Experienced investor. Hands-on. Not missing anything obvious.

Here's what the numbers showed.

**Insurance:** $340,000 annual premium across the portfolio. Last competitively placed in 2020, before the post-Ian repricing cycle. Direct carrier placement came back at $248,000. Saving: $92,000/year.

**Energy:** Two assets still on contracts from 2021. kWh rates 31% above current market. Cost to switch: zero. Annual saving after rollover: $41,000.

**Rents:** Three leases with review clauses. Two ERV gaps — 12% and 18% below market. Both review windows still open. Combined rent uplift on execution: $67,000/year.

**Additional income:** One light industrial unit with a rooftop qualifying for a solar PPA. Structured correctly, the PPA generates $14,000/year with no capital outlay from the owner.

Total annual value gap identified: $214,000.

At a 6.5% cap rate — roughly where Tampa Bay industrial and retail trades today — that's $3.3M in asset value. No acquisition. No development. No refinancing. Operational changes only.
