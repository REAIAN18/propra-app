## Engagement Protocol (Every Post)

1. **Post Monday–Thursday.** Friday posts get ~30% lower reach. Never post Saturday/Sunday for business content.
2. **No links in post body.** LinkedIn reduces distribution on posts with external URLs. CTAs say "link in profile" or "DM me."
3. **Reply within 2 hours.** LinkedIn's algorithm ranks early-engagement velocity. Reply to every comment within the first 2 hours — even a brief reply counts.
4. **Don't edit the post after publishing.** LinkedIn treats edits as resets for the algorithm window.
5. **Profile bio must be live.** Before Post 1 of any warmup sequence, ensure the signup link in your LinkedIn profile bio points to `https://realhq.com/signup`.

## Cross-Market Audience Logic

Ian's LinkedIn audience will be a mix of FL and SE UK commercial property owners. A post that's market-specific (FL insurance, SE UK MEES) won't confuse the other market — it reinforces that RealHQ operates in two specialist markets, which is a credibility signal for both audiences.

The general posts (Post 5, Post 7, Post 8) are designed to resonate with both markets and can be used as content calendar fillers if the schedule needs adjustment.
