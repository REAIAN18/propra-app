## Objection Handling (if needed)

**"I have a broker already"**
"Your broker earns commission from the carrier — they're incentivised to place you, not save you. We earn on the saving. Different incentive, different outcome."

**"How do I know these numbers are real?"**
"We show you the new carrier quote before you sign anything. The saving is on paper before you commit."

**"I'm not sure I want to share my financial data"**
"Start with just the insurance schedule — it's effectively public rate information. That alone usually finds the first saving, with nothing sensitive required."
