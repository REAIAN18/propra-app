## TOP 5 QUESTIONS + ANSWERS

**"How does pricing work?"**
> Commission-only. We earn a % of what we save or earn you. If we find nothing, you pay nothing. No monthly fee, no setup fee, no contract.

**"How does the AI work / is this just a chatbot?"**
> Live market data feeds — carrier quotes, energy tariffs, CoStar rental comps. AI surfaces the gaps; humans (us + our specialist network) execute the transactions. Not a report generator — we actually place the insurance, switch the energy.

**"What about data security?"**
> We handle insurance and energy data — less sensitive than a mortgage application. Hosted on [AWS/encrypted]. We can share our data handling policy if needed.

**"We already have a broker / managing agent — why do we need you?"**
> Brokers earn from carriers — incentive is placement, not saving. Managing agents don't do insurance retenders or energy switching. We're a different lane — we work alongside them.

**"How long until we see savings?"**
> Insurance: 3–4 weeks from audit to new placement. Energy: ~3 weeks. Rent review: depends on lease date, we prep the evidence now. Additional income: 4–8 weeks to survey and sign operator deal.

## TOP 3 OBJECTIONS + REBUTTALS

**"We already have an insurance broker"**
> "Brokers earn from carriers — their incentive is placement, not saving. We earn from your saving — opposite incentive. Most of our clients keep their broker for other things and use us for the competitive retender."

**"We manage this in-house / we're on top of it"**
> "That's great — most people I speak to feel the same way, and then we find 20–30% on insurance anyway because renewal ≠ retender. Takes 20 minutes to upload your schedule and find out. If we find nothing, it's cost you 20 minutes."

**"Not the right time"**
> "Totally fair. Two things don't wait well: lease review dates and insurance renewals. When's your next renewal? If it's in the next 60 days, it's worth a quick look now — we can have quotes back before it rolls."

## QUALIFYING QUESTIONS

- How many assets do you have? *(sweet spot: 3–30)*
- What mix — retail, industrial, office, mixed?
- Do you know your current insurance premium per asset?
- When did you last retender your energy contract? *(not renew — retender)*
- Any leases expiring or rent reviews due in the next 12 months?
- Do you have a managing agent or self-manage?

## WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE AT END OF CALL

**Best:** Prospect sends insurance schedule / energy contracts before EOD → health check initiated
**Good:** Agrees to send documents within the week → follow-up booked
**Minimum win:** Engagement letter signed → work begins

*If they say "send me more info" — send the one-pager + offer to book a follow-up. Don't just send a PDF and wait.*
