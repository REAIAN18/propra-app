## Personalisation guide

Before sending Email 1, spend 3 minutes:
- What asset types? Lead with the most relevant angle (industrial → energy; mixed-use → insurance + rent; office → rent review)
- What city? Use in subject line where possible
- Any recent acquisition or listed property? Reference it: "Saw your [asset type] in [city] — got me wondering..."
- How long have they held assets? Longer hold → more likely on legacy contracts → stronger insurance/energy angle
