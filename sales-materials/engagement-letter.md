# Arca — Engagement Letter Template

**When to send:** After a prospect confirms they want to proceed — typically when they're ready to share documents (insurance schedules, energy bills, lease summaries).

**Send as:** Plain email. No attachments. No header graphics. Just the text below.

---

## EMAIL TEMPLATE

**Subject:** Arca — next steps for [Company / Portfolio name]

---

[First name],

Great to speak with you. Here's a quick summary of how we work together from here.

**What Arca will do**

We'll run a full benchmarking analysis across your portfolio — insurance premiums against live market rates, energy costs against comparable assets, and passing rents against current achievable rents. You'll get a clear view of where you're overpaying or underearning, and what the realistic saving or uplift looks like.

**What we need from you**

To get started, please send over whichever of these you have:

- Insurance schedules (current policies, renewal dates, sums insured)
- Energy bills (last 12 months, or most recent available)
- Lease summaries or rent schedules (current passing rents, expiry dates, break clauses)

You don't need all three — send whatever you have and we'll work with it.

**Timeline**

Analysis back to you within 5 business days of receiving the documents.

**How we're paid**

No upfront fee, no retainer, no minimum charge.

Our fee is success-only:

- 15% of year-1 insurance saving
- 10% of year-1 energy saving
- 10% of year-1 rent uplift

Each fee becomes payable only when the relevant saving is contracted — new policy signed, new energy contract agreed, new lease or rent review settled. Either of us can walk away before that point with no obligation.

**To confirm**

Reply to this email and we'll get started.

[Signature]
