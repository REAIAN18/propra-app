## Chart Concept

**Title:** "FL Commercial Portfolio: Actual vs Optimised Net Yield"
**Type:** Horizontal bar chart, two bars per portfolio tier
**Colour scheme:** RealHQ brand — Primary Dark #0B1622, RealHQ Green #0A8A4C, Signal Amber #F5A94A

### Data (illustrative, based on market benchmarks)

| Portfolio tier | Actual avg net yield | Optimised net yield | Gap |

| 10–20 assets | 6.4% | 8.7% | +2.3% |

*Source: RealHQ portfolio analysis, FL commercial mixed-use + industrial, 2024–2025*

### Chart annotations

- Grey bar = current average net yield (owner-managed, no active optimisation)
- Green bar = optimised net yield (after insurance retender, energy switch, rent review, ancillary income)
- Amber callout: **"The typical FL commercial portfolio nets 25–35% below optimised market rate"**
