## PERSONALISATION GUIDE

Before sending, spend 2 minutes on their profile:
- How many properties visible? → reference the number
- What asset types? → lead with the most relevant hook (industrial = energy; mixed-use = insurance + rent)
- How long have they held assets? → older holds = more likely on legacy contracts
- Any recent acquisitions posted? → reference active buying as a signal they care about returns
- Mutual connections? → mention if relevant ("I see we're both connected to [X]")
