Every commercial portfolio has a gap between what it currently earns and what it should.

Most owners don't know the number. Arca does.

Here's what Arca runs against your portfolio, continuously:

Insurance — benchmarks your premium against current market rates for your asset type, sqft, and location. Flags when the gap is material and handles the retender.

Energy — maps your contracts against live tariffs for FL and SE UK markets. Identifies where you're on legacy pricing and recovers the difference.

Rent — tracks every lease against live ERV comparables. Flags reviews 12 months out with a documented comparable set, not a round estimate.

Ancillary income — surveys roof space, car parks, and vacant land for 5G, EV charging, solar, and subletting opportunities. Negotiates the terms.

Compliance — monitors certificate expiry dates (fire, gas, electrical) and flags 90 days before renewal. The average fine exposure per missed certificate in the UK: £6,000–£50,000.

Financing — stress-tests your portfolio against ICR thresholds and flags debt maturity risk before it becomes a problem.

Six areas. One dashboard. Commission-only — we earn from what we recover.
