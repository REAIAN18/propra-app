# RealHQ Referral Programme — Terms
*Share with: accountants, surveyors, solicitors, mortgage brokers, property managers*

---

## The Short Version

Introduce a commercial property owner to RealHQ. If we earn commission from them in the next 12 months, you earn 2% of everything we make. No cap. No admin. Paid monthly.

---

## Full Terms

**Programme name:** RealHQ Partner Referral Programme
**Effective date:** March 2026
**Operated by:** RealHQ (Ian Baron)

### 1. Who can participate
Any professional who introduces commercial property owners to RealHQ, including but not limited to:
- Chartered surveyors and valuation professionals
- Property solicitors and conveyancers
- Accountants (commercial property clients)
- Commercial mortgage brokers and lenders
- Financial advisers with property investor clients
- Property managers and managing agents

### 2. How to make a referral
Email Ian Baron at hello@realhq.com with:
- The client's name and company
- A brief note on their portfolio (asset count, types, location if known)
- OR copy Ian into an introduction email directly

### 3. Commission rate
**2% of all RealHQ commissions earned from the referred client, for the first 12 months from the date of first commission.**

No minimum. No maximum. Paid monthly alongside RealHQ's own commission settlements.

### 4. Example calculations

| RealHQ work | RealHQ commission | Your referral fee |
|---|---|---|
| Insurance retender, $18k saving | $2,700 (15%) | $54 |
| Energy switch, $12k saving | $1,200 (10%) | $24 |
| Rent review, $15k uplift | $1,200 (8%) | $24 |
| 5G mast, $10k/yr income | $1,000 (10% yr1) | $20 |
| **Full portfolio, year 1** | **$6,100** | **$122** |

On a larger portfolio (15+ assets):

| RealHQ work | RealHQ commission | Your referral fee |
|---|---|---|
| Full portfolio year-1 | $22,000 | $440 |

### 5. Commission eligibility
- Referral must be made before RealHQ has engaged with the client independently
- Client must sign an RealHQ engagement letter for commission to qualify
- Referral fee applies to commissions received in the 12-month window only (not future years)
- Referral fees are paid in the same currency and timing as RealHQ's own receipts

### 6. Transparency
RealHQ will share a monthly summary of commissions earned from your referred clients, showing the calculation basis for your referral fee.

### 7. No exclusivity
Partners may refer clients to other services. RealHQ may also work with clients independently. The referral programme does not create an exclusive relationship.

### 8. Variation
RealHQ may update these terms with 30 days' notice. Commission rates are fixed for the 12-month referral window at the rate in effect at the date of introduction.
